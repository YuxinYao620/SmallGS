Troubleshooting Notes:

  • Videos should be encoded with H.264 (AVC) video codec and AAC audio codec for maximum browser compatibility
  • For MP4, use profiles that are widely supported (Baseline, Main)
  • Check browser console for specific errors (Right-click > Inspect > Console)
  • WebM format (VP8/VP9 with Vorbis/Opus audio) is an alternative format for browsers that don't support MP4
